The Problem

Why Elderly Safety Matters

Every year, millions of elderly individuals face life-threatening falls at home. Our design thinking journey began with understanding this critical issue.

0 Happen at home

The Silent Crisis

Elderly individuals often experience reduced balance, muscle strength, and slower reflexes, making them highly prone to falls inside the house — especially in bathrooms, kitchens, and near beds.

When they live alone, a fall can leave them unable to get up or call for help, leading to serious injuries, health complications, or worse. The fear of falling again reduces their confidence and limits their mobility.

⚠️

Critical Insight

Bathrooms are the most dangerous place — wet and slippery floors cause the majority of indoor falls among seniors.

Final Product

SafeGuard Smart Watch

A wearable fall detection device that combines motion sensing, multi-stage confirmation, and instant emergency alerts.

Intelligent Protection

Our Smart Fall Detection Watch uses an MPU6050 accelerometer and gyroscope to continuously monitor body motion. When a sudden fall-like motion is detected, it triggers a 10-second warning with sound and vibration.

  • 📡

    Real-time Motion Tracking

    Detects abnormal acceleration, impact, and inactivity patterns

  • ⏱️

    10-Second Cancel Window

    User can cancel false alerts before emergency is sent

  • 📱

    Instant SMS Alerts

    GSM module sends emergency messages to caregivers

  • 🔋

    Long Battery Life

    Rechargeable Li-ion battery with low-battery warnings
