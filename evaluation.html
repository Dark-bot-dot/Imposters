How we evaluated 108 ideas and shortlisted the most feasible, desirable, and viable concept.

Criteria

Evaluation Matrix

Why the Watch Won

The Smart Fall Detection Watch scored highest because it is wearable (works everywhere, not just bathroom/bedroom), familiar (elderly already wear watches), and reliable (multi-stage confirmation reduces false alarms).

Analysis

Why We Rejected Other Ideas

01

Floor Mat

Only works in one location. Elderly can fall anywhere — stairs, kitchen, outside. Limited coverage was a dealbreaker.

02

Grab Bars

Requires bathroom renovation. Not portable. Many elderly don't have modern bathrooms. Installation is complex and expensive.

03

AI Home Hub

Too complex for a student prototype. Requires WiFi, cloud processing, and voice recognition — beyond our scope and resources.

04

Walking Stick

Not all elderly use sticks. Stigma attached. Can be forgotten or left behind. Doesn't monitor when user is resting.

Final Decision

SafeGuard Smart Watch

After rigorous evaluation, we unanimously chose the Wearable Smart Fall Detection Watch as our final solution.

Winning Factors:

  • 🌎

    Universal Coverage

    Works everywhere — bathroom, stairs, outdoors. Not limited to one room.

  • 👍

    No Stigma

    Looks like a normal watch. Elderly already comfortable wearing watches.

  • ⚡

    Instant Alerts

    GSM module sends SMS within seconds. No dependency on smartphone or WiFi.

  • 🎓

    Student-Feasible

    Uses Arduino, MPU6050, and SIM800L — all components we can learn and build.
